Our Foundation

New York's Most Trusted Chauffeur Service

Grand Via Luxe was founded with a singular vision: to bring European-standard luxury ground transportation to the streets of New York. Since our founding, we have grown from a boutique three-vehicle operation to a full-fleet luxury transport company trusted by executives, celebrities, dignitaries, and discerning families across the tri-state area.

Our name — Grand Via Luxe — embodies the philosophy we operate by. Every route is a "Grand Via" — a great path — and we execute each journey with the finest standard of "Luxe" service. From the moment a client makes contact with us to the moment they step out of our vehicle, excellence is non-negotiable.

Based in New York City, we service all five boroughs, New Jersey, Connecticut, and Long Island — with specialist airport services at JFK, LaGuardia, Newark, and Westchester airports.
